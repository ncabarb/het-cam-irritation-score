HET-CAM Irritation Score

If you use this tool, please cite:

Lima, N.C.B. (2026). Web-based calculator for HET-CAM irritation score. Adapted from Luepke (1985). Developed by Natália Cavalcante – PhD Student in Pharmacology, Federal University of Ceará (UFC).

This tool performs deterministic calculations and does not store or modify user data. All computations occur locally in the user's browser. This calculator is intended as a supportive analytical tool and does not replace the HET-CAM experimental assay. Results should be interpreted in conjunction with experimental observations and expert judgment.
